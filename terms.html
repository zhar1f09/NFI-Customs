Order Terms of Service

Please review the following terms before placing an order. These guidelines ensure fairness, efficiency, and professionalism in every transaction.

01. Refunds & Cancellations

Refunds are only considered under reasonable circumstances and must be mutually agreed upon by both client and designer. Credits may be issued if approved by HR. Minor delays do not qualify for refunds unless deemed critical. Orders canceled before 70% completion require 50% payment; after 70% completion, full payment is required.

02. Order Details

Clients must provide all necessary information required for the designer to fulfill the order. Missing or incomplete details may delay production.

03. Payment Policy

Payment is required upon opening an order. Designers will not begin work until payment has been confirmed with sufficient proof of purchase.

04. Completion Time

Delivery timelines vary depending on workload and complexity. Clients are expected to provide order details promptly to avoid delays.

05. Finalized Sales

Once a product has been delivered and confirmed, the sale is final. No refunds or cancellations will be issued after 5–7 business days.

06. Designer Pinging

Excessive or repeated pinging of designers will result in immediate ticket closure. Refunds will not be issued in such cases.

Not all regulations are listed here — use common sense. For inquiries, please open a support ticket.
